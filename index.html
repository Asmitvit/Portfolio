Gamification of Fintech: Unveiling the Psychological Strategies of Fintech Apps

March-2024

This research explores how modern fintech apps exploit psychological principles—like loss aversion, social validation, and reward systems—to influence user behavior and drive impulsive financial decisions. It investigates gamification elements such as cashback, scratch cards, and visual cues that mimic casino-like experiences to maximize user engagement. The study also examines the evolution of these techniques and provides recommendations for individual awareness and policy-level interventions to mitigate financial manipulation.
